Kdes, holubičko, lítala

Posted on 2017-02-28 by xkollar in Music.

Another étude-like folk piece.

In case you are not familiar with it, you can get the feel at https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=e1m5k8d-t7k.

Notes:

  • Pay attention to dynamics.
  • Dynamics change between 8th and 9th bar should be audible but smooth (not too intense).
  • Train hands separately, with left extra attention to timing (count…)
  • Once comfortable, start putting things together.
